MY BACKGROUND

During a job event back in 2018, I came across DataCamp, an online platform to learn data analytics and data science. After successfully completing DataCamp's Data Scientist Career Track, I have become involved in the data field.

I hold a Master of Science (MSc.) in Management from Vrije Universiteit Brussel in Belgium (2019). I believe my experience in business and my master's degree empower me to see the big picture and ask the right analytical questions to deliver the right business solutions.

As a self-taught data scientist, I have been able to complete a lot of diverse set of projects I am showcasing on this website. I am convinced that learning from a project and on-job learning is the best experience to learn from.

My interest in coding and software has a longer history. I have been using Linux for more than a decade as a home user and I am also familiar with HTML, CSS and basics of JavaScript. Next, I am willing to learn Apache Spark (PySpark) and Scala for Big Data.I am also a fan of fastAI, a platform promoting the state-of-the-art in Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
